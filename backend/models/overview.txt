Separation of Concerns:

    Keeps data-related code separate from other components.

Schema Definition:

Encapsulation:
    Allows grouping related functionality together.

Reusability:
    Models can be easily reused across different parts of the application.
    Enhances modularity and code reuse.

Clarity and Readability:

    Makes it easier for developers to navigate and maintain the codebase.
